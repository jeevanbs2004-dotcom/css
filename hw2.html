Vivo X200 Pro

The Vivo X200 Pro features a 6.78-inch AMOLED display, the MediaTek Dimensity 9400 processor, and a triple rear camera system with 50MP wide, 50MP ultra-wide, and 200MP telephoto lenses. It includes a 6000mAh battery with 90W fast charging, 16GB of RAM, up to 1TB of storage, and runs on Android 15.
